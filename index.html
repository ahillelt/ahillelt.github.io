About

Bridging academic rigor with real-world industry impact

Academic Mission

As Industry Assistant Professor at NYU Tandon School of Engineering, I teach graduate courses in cybersecurity and computer networking, mentor students through the OSIRIS Lab, and conduct research at the intersection of hardware security, cryptographic systems, AI governance, and AI safety.

My research explores physical hardware security, computational intelligence, and the societal implications of emerging technologies. Drawing on my background in conflict resolution from Columbia's Earth Institute, I examine how technical systems interact with human decision-making and governance structures.

Research Focus

AI Governance & Policy: Developing frameworks for responsible AI deployment, examining governance mechanisms for AI systems, and advising on regulatory approaches that balance innovation with societal safety.

Teaching Philosophy

Security education must be experiential and hands-on. Students learn best by breaking systems, building defenses, and understanding the socio-technical context in which technology operates.

Industry Leadership

Over 18 years in emerging technology, entrepreneurship, regulation, and public policy. Co-founded RocketHub, one of the first global crowdfunding platforms, guiding it to acquisition in 2015 after facilitating over $50M in funding across 30,000+ projects.

As Managing Partner at Stacked Capital, invested in deep-tech ventures focusing on hardware security, AI infrastructure, and critical systems resilience.

Testified twice before U.S. House subcommittees on the JOBS Act implementation, and co-authored three whitepapers on crowdfunding regulation submitted to Congress, SEC, and FINRA.

Consulting & Advisory

Available for strategic consulting on security architecture, threat modeling, applied cryptography, and building security-conscious engineering cultures.

Research & Publications

Applied security research addressing real-world challenges

Hardware & Physical Security

Fault injection attacks, side-channel analysis, hardware trojans, and practical defenses for embedded systems and IoT devices in resource-constrained environments.

Applied Cryptography

Misuse-resistant cryptographic APIs, secure protocol design, and making strong cryptography accessible to developers through secure-by-default systems.

AI Governance & Safety

Governance frameworks for responsible AI deployment, operational safety in AI systems, regulatory policy development, adversarial robustness, and designing decision-support tools aligned with human values. Combining technical expertise with conflict resolution methodologies to address AI alignment challenges.

Critical Infrastructure

Failure modes in complex socio-technical systems, graceful degradation under attack, and recovery strategies for essential services.

Security Governance

Policy frameworks, standards development, and incentive structures that encourage responsible innovation and security investment.

Education at Scale

Curriculum design, hands-on lab development, and making advanced security concepts accessible to working professionals.
